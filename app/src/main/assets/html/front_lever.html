Front Lever

 

Technique Summary:

- Straight arms

- Elbows locked out

- Scapula protracted

- Body parallel to the ground

- Hips on level with shoulders

 

Description:

Execution:
The Front Lever is an advanced Calisthenics skill, where you hold the back of your body parallel to the ground, supported only through your arms. Your arms should be straight and your core tightened. Retract your scapula (push shoulders back) and keep your hips high (on level with shoulders).
