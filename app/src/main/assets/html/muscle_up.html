Muscle Up

 

Technique Summary:

- Pull behind bar

- Keep elbows in

- Rotate your wrists over the bar

 

Description:

The Muscle-Up can be divided into 3 phases:

1. Pull
2. Transition
3. Push

First you pull your body as high as you can (chest or even hip on level with bar). The probably toughest part is the transition. To succeed you'll need to rotate your wrists on top of the bar and throw your shoulders over the bar. The last step is a bar Dip.

The Muscle-up needs almost all muscles in your upper body. It needs explosivness and a good coordination of the muscles. The Muscle-up can also be performed with gymnastic rings. On the rings you'll often see a false grip (start dead hang with wrists over rings).
